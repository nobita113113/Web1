The Land Of Openness And Cultural Diversity

Gordon Ramsay, the world-renowned British chef and recipient of 16 prestigious Michelin stars, once remarked, “Vietnamese cuisine is truly unique and cannot be compared to any dish in the countries I have visited” while enjoying Hu Tieu on a boat drifting along a river in the Southwest region of Vietnam. Following the journey of this celebrated chef, it becomes clear that Vietnam offers not only the iconic Pho, but also Hu Tieu, a dish equally rich in flavor and tradition.
